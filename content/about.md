Hello there! I am a science enthusiast and software engineer with over ten years of experience building data-driven applications across a variety of industries. Since 2016, I have been working with Python, TypeScript, Rust, and Java to develop and deploy microservices, backend systems, and data-dirven applications.

I hold a BS in Mechanical Engineering and an MS in Mechatronics from [FH Aachen](https://www.fh-aachen.de/), Germany. This engineering background gives me a practical understanding of both technical systems and real-world business processes — a foundation I bring to every project.

My career began in research and development at [Fraunhofer IPT](https://www.ipt.fraunhofer.de/en.html), where I worked on bringing innovative technologies from the lab to market together with industry partners. Over the last nine years, I have delivered software projects for companies such as Deutsche Telekom, Otto Group, Telekom Außendienst, and Atlas Copco.

Today, my focus is on helping companies take their first — or next — steps with **Agentic AI**. I work with organizations to integrate AI agents into their existing workflows and connect them securely to their enterprise data — respecting authorization levels, access controls, and data segregation across teams and roles — turning complex, manual processes into intelligent, automated pipelines.

Whether you are exploring where AI agents can add value or ready to build your first production system, I am here to help you get started.

I was accepted in [Mensa](https://www.mensa.org/) in 2018.
